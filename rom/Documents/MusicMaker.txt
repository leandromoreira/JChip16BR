=========================
 "MusicMaker" for Chip16:
=========================
 
 Author: Shendo
 Date: 05/15/2011

========
 About:
========

 A program which allows you to create
 simple music with Chip16's SPU.
 
===========
 Controls:
===========
 
 D-PAD - Move selector.
 START - Play/Stop. 
 A - Activate block.
 B - Erase column.
