==========================
 "Reflection" for Chip16:
==========================
 
 Author: Shendo
 Date: 04/30/2011

========
 About:
========

 The goal of this game is to lead a beam of light from start (bulb) to finish (crystal).
 To achieve that goal you must use mirrors scattered across the field.
 
===========
 Controls:
===========
 
 D-PAD - Move selector.
 A - flip mirror.
