======================
 "Herdle" for Chip16:
======================
 
 Author: Kelsall Bros.
 Date: 04/16/2011

========
 About:
========

 The object of this game is to reach the gate at the
 top of the screen without crashing.
 
 As the game progresses the character moves
 more quickly thus increasing difficulty.
 
===========
 Controls:
===========
 
 D-PAD - Move character accross the screen.
